TRANSLATE VIDEOS ONLINE
As digital communication becomes more visual, we propose that a system that can automatically translate a video of a person speaking in one language into a target language with realistic lip synchronisation is required. The desire to add a visual module into a translation system stems from the fact that the majority of today's information stream is becoming audio-visual. Existing systems can only translate such audiovisual content at the speech-to-speech level, posing significant constraints. As a result, we extend existing face-to-face translation systems by proposing a pipeline that can take a video of a person speaking in the source language and output a video of the same speaker speaking in the target language, with the voice style and lip movements justifying the destination language.
HOW IT WORKS

STEP 01

Click on 'Choose Video' to add a video file. Drag and drop your video into the box to choose it.

STEP 02

Choose the language you wish to translate into and the gender of the speaker in your uploaded video! For the time being, our system supports Hindi, Bengali, Telugu, and Nepali language. Select either male or female as the speaker.

STEP 03

Press the Translate button to start our AI's magic! Your desired translated video will show up within a few minutes.
